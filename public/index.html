The Association Powering Profitability, Scale, and Investability

Don’t predict the future. Let’s go out and build it!

The Crays Business Nomads Association is structured as a Swiss non-profit to provide a legally robust and neutral framework for governing a global network of operators, investors, and partners. It enables shared access to opportunities such as capital markets, real estate, and venture funding while partners remain economically independent and benefit from shared infrastructure, demand, and network effects.

Operating architecture

Intent becomes matching, access, booking and settlement.

Crays helps guests, users, members and partners move from intention to real-world access: people signal what they need, smart matching connects them with the right places and people, booking rails handle the next step, and Bitcoin-native payments can settle value when it makes sense.

Demand loopFrom signal to booked demand

Profiles, venue context and community intent become demand signals. Matching routes them to people, rooms, events or services; execution handles booking or access; settlement records value through payments, perks or partner workflows.

Who should join

A member model for people who want to use, build, fund or operate the ecosystem.

Crays is built as a Swiss association in Zug for individuals, companies and project partners who want to support, use or grow the ecosystem. Depending on the role, members can join community, core, investor or passive participation paths and access events, training and services offered by the association and local Crays Clubs.

Community Group

For active business nomads, VIP members, guests, creators, travelers and people who want Crays as their global home.

Core Group

For founders, operators, project leads, governance contributors and specialists who help build the association and its projects.

Investor Group

For members and partners who provide capital, fund local Crays Clubs or help maintain companies and real-world assets.

Passive Members

For supporters, local club communities and partner audiences who want access and participation without voting governance.

Governance without losing momentum

The association can use DAO mechanisms, council decisions, digital voting and eventually token-based proof of membership to coordinate internal organisation, standards, project approvals and participation while remaining anchored in Swiss law.
