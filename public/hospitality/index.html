Retail, access and invisible checkout

Your hospitality spaces can earn from every useful interaction, not only from rooms and drinks.

Inside a Crays venue, every useful interaction can become part of the business model: physical products in retail zones, F&B and coworking areas billed by time or usage, plus digital products and services that only exist inside the Crays app. The venue becomes a living commerce layer driven by community demand, not a static shop in the corner.

With a wallet-linked membership NFT, you can check into a Crays Club, unlock access, open rooms, use coworking time, take products from shelves, receive offers and access digital services. When you leave, the session can settle automatically and your receipt is ready for review.
